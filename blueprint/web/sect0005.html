  • Rudin.Ch05.stone_weierstrass

If \(X\) is a compact Hausdorff space and \(A\) is a real subalgebra of \(C(X,\mathbb {R})\) that separates points, then \(A\) is dense in \(C(X,\mathbb {R})\).
