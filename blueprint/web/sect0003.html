Let \(E\) be a real vector space, \(N\colon E \to \mathbb {R}\) a sublinear functional, and \(f\) a linear functional defined on a subspace of \(E\) with \(f \le N\) on its domain. Then \(f\) extends to a linear functional \(g\colon E \to \mathbb {R}\) with \(g \le N\) everywhere.
